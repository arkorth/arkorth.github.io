Peanut Butter Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • Plain Oats
  • Milk of Choice
  • Chia Seeds
  • Powdered Peanut Butter
  • Any other ingredients can be added to this recipe, but the measurements, espcially the milk, may need be be adjusted

Cooking Tools Needed:

  • Mason Jar / Container
  • Spoon
  • Measuring Utensils

Recipe:

  • 1. Pour 1/2 C of oats in mason jar
  • 2. Add 1 C of milk
  • 3. Add 2 TBSP powdered peanut butter
  • 4. Add 1 TBSP chia seeds
  • 5. Mix all of the ingredients together in the mason jar or container
  • 6. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
